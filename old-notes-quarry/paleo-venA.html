Venetus A: Paleography Notes of the Main Text

Ligatures

Ligatures This section includes examples of commonly seen ligatures (combinations of two or more letters) in the main Iliadic text of the Venetus A.

Ligatures starting with epsilon

Ligatures starting with epsilon Some of the most common, and initially most troubling, ligatures are the frequent combinations with epsilon:
  1. epsilon-epsilon, ἐελδωρ
  2. epsilon-iota, Ἀτρειδης
  3. epsilon-nu, ἐτισεν
  4. epsilon-omicron, ἐρεοντο
  5. epsilon-pi, δεπας
  6. epsilon-sigma, εἰπες
  7. epsilon-sigma, ἐστι
  8. epsilon-sigma, φρεσι
  9. epsilon-tau-omicron, ᾠχετο
  10. epsilon-upsilon, ἐκλυες
  11. epsilon-chi, ἐχων
